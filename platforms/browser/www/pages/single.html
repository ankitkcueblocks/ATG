Requirements to grow

Requirements to grow

  • Spot

    Choose your sunniest spot for growing okra as it grows best in light (at least 5-6 hours of sunlight is essential)

  • Soil

    Okra grows best in soil with a near-neutral pH between 6.5 and 7.0. The soil should be loamy, crumbly and well drained. Apply a layer of mulch 4 to 8 inches high. How to Harvest

How to Harvest

  • 1

    Okra will be ready to harvest in 2 months after plantation. It should be harvested when it is 2-4 inches long and can be harvested every other day. Harvest the pods as they are tender or they will become tough and stringy if left on the plant for too long.

  • 2

    Cut leaves from the outer portions of the plant whenever you need them, the inner portions of the plant can be left to mature.

  • 3

    Store the uncut and uncooked pods in freezer bags and put them in a freezer.
